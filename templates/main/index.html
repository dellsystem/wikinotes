Find a course.

Enter a course name in the box in the top right corner, or browse our selection of courses:

  • Browse by faculty
  • Browse by department
  • Browse by semester
  • Browse by professor
  • View popular courses

Learn and study.

Catch up on lectures you missed with lecture notes or course summaries. Quiz yourself with our multiple choice or vocabulary quizzes. Do a past exam and compare your answers to the student-made or official solutions. Read course reviews and tips written by other students.

Contribute.

Give back to the community by adding lecture notes, editing content or even just spreading the word. If you're interested in contributing or becoming a moderator, please register and check out our contributor guidelines.

Not sure where to start?

First, watch our video »

Then:

  • Learn about us and what we're doing
  • Fork us on github and send us a pull request
  • Join our #wikinotes IRC channel on freenode
  • Find a random page and make it better

Found a bug or broken link? Have feature suggestions? Want to sponsor us? Let us know! You can email us at admin this domain, mention us on Twitter, or write on the wall of our Facebook page.

Alternatively, if you have money you want to throw at us, we're not going to say no; see our donations page to find out how to donate and where the money would go to.
